Hi, I'm Eli

I'm a Philadelphia-based User Experience Designer and Researcher

Projects

As a designer, I've worked on various projects that have tap into my creative spirit and work towards giving a personal visual experience to every digtial product I design. As a UX researcher, I am passionate about exploring different subjects and experiences through a humanistic perspective.

El Taco

For my user interface design class, we were tasked with redesigning an existing website. I chose El Taco because of its great business presence in the community, while its current website was lacking visually.

STAR Research

My 2022 Summer, I participated in Drexel's STAR (Students Tackling Advanced Research) program. My research project, "Principles of Accessible Game Design: Making Video Games Inclusive to Players on the Autism Spectrum".

CoSign

CoSign is a mobile app meant to make learning basic American Sign Language (ASL) easy and fun to learn. Through interactive lessons taught by Deaf instructors, users can pick up conversational ASL to connect and communicate with other ASL users.
